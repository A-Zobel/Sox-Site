Poetry Night
Live Event

A weekly evening for spoken word and stories.

Enjoy a warm, intimate atmosphere built for poets, readers, and everyone who loves language. Each session highlights local voices, open mic slots, and community conversation.

Event details

Every second Thursday at 7pm

Bring a poem, bring a friend, or simply take in the performance. The night is relaxed and welcoming, with coffee service and soft seating designed for conversation.

What to expect

  • Open mic readings and featured guest performances
  • Prompt-based creative exercises to inspire new work
  • Light snacks and specialty coffee available

Who can join

This event is open to all experience levels. If you’re curious about performance or just want to enjoy a quiet creative evening, Poetry Night is for you.
