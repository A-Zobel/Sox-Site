Book Club

A literary evening for curious readers.

Our Book Club meets once each month in a calm setting, designed to welcome meaningful discussion and shared discoveries over coffee and conversation.

What happens at book club?

We gather around a chosen title, share thoughts, and talk about themes, characters, and moments that stayed with us. Expect a relaxed pace and a friendly, open-minded group.

Every meeting includes a short prompt, optional reading rounds, and time to recommend new books for future sessions.

  • Monthly featured book
  • Warm drinks and conversation
  • Inclusive space for all readers

Next meeting

First Tuesday of the month at 6:30pm. Arrive early to grab a seat and a cup of coffee.

Bring along

Your thoughts, a favourite quote, and a curious mind. No prior preparation is required.
