Game format

Blood on the Clocktower is a social deduction event where players take on hidden roles and work to uncover the truth. The energy is intense, imaginative, and always collaborative.

What to expect

Roleplay Each player receives a secret character with special abilities.
Story A living narrative unfolds as the community votes, debates, and reveals clues. Different scripts can lead to unique stories and priorities
Atmosphere A dramatic night with twists, turns, and unexpected revelations.

How to join

Reserve your seat through the form at the top, or email admin@murphyst.com for more information. You can also find us on social media for updates and announcements as Jordan (@harkmatter) and Alex (@alex.zobel.5)
